We worked on the design and layout of RENTACAR website, a company with a long history that offers car rentals in Argentina

The client's requirements were to have a clean and easy-to-navigate website so that the user experience is simple and intuitive, thus achieving a good relationship with the functionality of the site. For this we base on trying to achieve a simple vehicle search that shows results with the necessary information to make the reservation and a quick and specific rental flow.
